Anagrab

Welcome to Anagrab. Your goal is to work your way through a stack of five words as quickly as possible by turning all or some letters of the word into new words below. Press to enter your word. The clock is ticking! If you run out of anagrams to make, you can skip to the next word, but each "orphaned" tile will count for a fifteen second penalty! Press start to begin.
