Anagrab

In Anagrab, you turn a word into new words! Press to enter your word. If you run out of anagrams to make, you can skip to the next word, but each tile left behind will be counted as an "orphan." In timed mode, you will get a daily stack of five words to work through, with each orphan tile counting as a fifteen second penalty as you work against the clock! In endurance mode, you will get an endless supply of random words, but the game is over once you accumulate more than four orphans! Please select timed or endurance mode below.
